About: The Prehistory of the Future

Published on October 21, 2024 at 3:53 PM

OmniSphere.

OmniSphere is a project exploring the boundary between virtual reality and the real world. Through articles and multimedia resources, it questions the consequences of technological advances, particularly virtual reality and artificial intelligence, in the near future.
I am Bryan THIERRY, a first-year student in the MMI program at Montbéliard. For this project on "The Prehistory of the Future", I chose the VR headset, a symbol of innovation but also of questions about dependency, the authenticity of human interactions, and the link between the real and virtual worlds. This theme echoes the excessive use of social networks and its effects on our relationships and health. The design of the site, with a futuristic aesthetic inspired by Ready Player One and pink and blue colors, reflects the immersive universe of virtual reality.

Glossary

  • Virtual Reality: Technology that creates immersive environments in which the user can interact.
  • Artificial Intelligence: System capable of simulating human intelligence processes, such as learning and decision-making.
  • Dystopian: Related to an imaginary society where living conditions are extremely bad, often due to tyranny or environmental degradation.
  • Simulation: Representation of a system or process that allows analyzing its behavior.
  • Multiverse: Theoretical concept according to which multiple universes can exist in parallel with different realities.
  • NPC: Non-player character, controlled by the game, interacting with players.
